# Hunting Grounds: Source List

This file contains the primary sources for discovering "Blue Ocean" video topics.

## 1. Government Conspiracies & Declassified Secrets
*   **The Black Vault (theblackvault.com):** The largest private repository of declassified government documents.
    *   *Focus:* UFOs, CIA experiments, military tech.
*   **CIA CREST (cia.gov/readingroom):** The CIA's declassified archives.
    *   *Focus:* Cold War espionage, MKULTRA offshoots, psychic research (Stargate).
*   **FBI The Vault (vault.fbi.gov):** FBI files on historical figures and crimes.
    *   *Focus:* Organized crime, unexplained phenomena, domestic terrorism.
*   **National Security Archive (nsarchive.gwu.edu):** High-level diplomatic and military secrets.
    *   *Focus:* Nuclear history, coups, foreign policy disasters.

## 2. Corporate Fraud & Collusion
*   **Retraction Watch (retractionwatch.com):** Tracks scientific papers being retracted.
    *   *Story Angle:* "The Scientist Who Faked Everything."
*   **FTC Press Release Archive:** Old actions against companies for bizarre scams.
*   **"The Old Internet" Scams:** Searching specific Wayback Machine snapshots of "Get Rich Quick" forums from 1999-2005.

## 3. The "Dark Side" of the Internet & Tech
*   **Usenet Archives (Google Groups / endless.horse):** The internet before the web.
    *   *Search:* "flamewar", "virus source code", "hackers" (dated < 1995).
*   **TextFiles.com:** The culture of BBS and early internet anarchy.
*   **Exploit Database (exploit-db.com):** History of major digital vulnerabilities.

## 4. Paranormal & High Strangeness
*   **Fortean Times Archives:** The gold standard for "weird news."
*   **Newspapers.com (Local Search):** Searching small-town papers for "strange lights", "monster sightings", or "mass hysteria" events.
*   **The Charles Fort Institute:** Anomalous phenomena data.

## 5. Human Interest / Incredible Circumstances
*   **Wikipedia "Unusual Articles" Page:** A curated list of the weirdest verified pages.
*   **DamnInteresting.com:** Long-form articles about obscure history (check for ones NOT covered on YT).
*   **Obituaries of the "Eccentric":** Searching for keywords like "recluse," "inventor," "survived."

## Search Strategies (Dorking)
*   `site:gov filetype:pdf "top secret" -unclassified`
*   `site:archive.org "index of" "confidential"`
*   `"forgotten" OR "obscure" site:reddit.com/r/UnresolvedMysteries -site:youtube.com`
